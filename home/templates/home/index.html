Let's bring ideas to life!

Whether you want to create a game, or want to contribute to what you think games should be like these days, don't hesitate to get started with this platform, donating to projects of interest or setting up a donation flow for that great game of yours that needs support to make it a reality!

What is Gamestarter

Purpose

This platform was created to give a platform for videos games to have a chance of being kickstarted - by the gamers, for gamers

Goal

To have a community where game developers and game lovers can share ideas and support one another

In Practical Terms?

A simple donation platform, enabling users to donate to game projects/ideas, and allows creators to share their projects/ideas

How it Works

Want to Contribute?

Just register, have a look through game projects listed, donate to those that interest you, and follow updates via the project's blog

A Game Developer/Studio?

Register as a creator, add a project to the project list, and be sure to update donars about development via the blog feature

Blog and Rewards (Coming Soon)

Creators will be able to communicate with donars through a blog feature as well as be able to communicate rewards
